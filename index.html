I'm a postdoc at King's College London with a research interest in geometric analysis and applications of differential geometry to machine learning. I previously studied for a PhD at Imperial College where my advisors were Jason Lotay (University of Oxford) and Sir Simon Donaldson (Imperial College). (At the time of writing I had not obtained my PhD.)
